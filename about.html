About Our Company

Brentwood Logistics & Security Company, S.A. (BLS) is a specialized provider of armed and unarmed Bank Protection Officers operating throughout the Republic of Panamá. Founded in 2008, we deliver compliance-driven protective services tailored to financial institutions, commercial facilities, and government-regulated environments.

With nationwide operational support and structured administrative processes for security clearances, hiring, and compliance, we ensure that every officer assigned to your location meets strict professional and regulatory standards. Our approach enhances both security performance and customer-facing professionalism — adding prestige and value while deterring risks.

Through advanced security management systems and strategic technology partnerships, we maintain accountability, transparency, and measurable service quality across every branch we protect.
