We help you answer these questions.

We analyze and implement a security guard plan tailored to your specific institution needs, developing guidelines with the expectation that the identified risks will be addressed.

Brentwood Security Auditing Services include developing, implementing and evaluating field protection controls, including walk-in/walk-out traffic, integrated perimeter and interior detection, access control, video surveillance and fire protection and validation of site safeguards and security method, policies, procedures, manuals orders and protection strategies.

Our security auditing involves three steps:

ASSESSMENT

Independent and comprehensive assessment of existing Safety and Security infrastructure including security staff and security resources.

IDENTIFICATION

Most significant and critical Safety and Security risks and threats are identified.

ACTION PLAN

A detailed report is prepared covering Safety and Security system risk exposure and threats discovered with suggestions for their effective elimination.
